    \caption{\textbf{d$_N$/d$_S$ and abundance of positively selected genes.} 
    \textit{Violin plots}: Pairwise comparisons between members of three species groups with violin plots of d$_N$/d$_S$ values 
    for X-linked and autosomal 1-to-1 orthologs, and scatterplots of d$_S$ vs. d$_N$. Permutation tests (n=10000) show that 
    d$_N$/d$_S$ is significantly lower for X-linked orthologs in all comparisons within \textit{Bruchini} (\textbf{A}) and 
    \textit{Coccinella} (\textbf{B}), but not \textit{Tribolium} (\textbf{C}) (see Fig. S7 for permutation test results). 
    \textit{Bar plots}: 
    The bar plot show the percentage of genes that have codons under positive selection according to paml site-model analysis 
    (likelihood ratio test comparison between paml site models M1a and M2a). Permutation tests (n=10000) show that 
    \textit{Bruchini} (\textbf{A}) have an enrichment of positively selected X-linked orthologs in all pairwise comparisons, 
    while \textit{Coccinella} (\textbf{B}) and \textit{Tribolium} (\textbf{C}) show no significant difference (see Fig. S8).
    }\label{dS_plots}
